I've spent the last 8 years designing B2B SaaS products: design systems, analytics platforms, and AI-powered tools. I work in problem spaces where the UX patterns don't exist yet and need to be invented rather than applied.

I work at the intersection of design and engineering: I prototype in code, contribute to component libraries, and think in design tokens rather than pixel specs. The tools change (Figma, SwiftUI, React, Framer) but the practice stays the same: understand the user, build the right abstraction, iterate until it's invisible.

I've shipped products used by teams at companies ranging from early-stage startups to 500-person orgs. I'm looking for my next challenge: somewhere I can work on hard problems with people who care about craft.
